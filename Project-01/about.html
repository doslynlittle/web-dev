A.A. Milne was an english author known fr writing the books Winie the Pooh. He was born 18 January 1882. His actual name is Alan Alexander Milne. A.A. Milne Started of as a play writer befor he became known for writing winnie the Pooh. The inspiration behind Winnie the Pooh was A.A. Milnes son Christopher Robin Milne,He was inspired when he went to the zoo with Christopher. Christopher was intrigued by the tamed bear at the zoo, this is what sparked A.A. Milnes inspiration to write the book. Before A.A. Milne became an author he was in the British Army in World War I and served as an officer in the Royal Warwickshire Regiment and later, after a debilitating illness, the Royal Corps of Signals. He was commissioned into the 4th Battalion, Royal Warwickshire Regiment, on 1 February 1915. A.A. Milne went through a lot of life changes befor becoming the famous author he is today.
